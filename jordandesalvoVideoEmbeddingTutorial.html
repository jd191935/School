How to Embed a Video by Jordan Desalvo

Step 1: Find the Video

Go to the video you want to embed on the video hosting service.

Step 2: Click on the Share Button

Click on the 'Share' button below the video.

Step 3: Copy the Embed Code

Click on 'Embed' and then copy the embed code provided.

Step 4: Paste the Embed Code

Paste the embed code into your HTML file where you want the video to appear.
